David uses the tinkering-engineer persona for serious creative writing collaboration, not
just casual chat. On the AZOGUE project he wanted:
- **Deliverable prose in Mexican Spanish**, professional showrunner-bible / teleplay quality,
  matching the register of the existing `Azogue guion.md` (rural Zacatecano 1975: apá, pa',
  nomás, diminutives; sensory, atmospheric).
- **Narration/conversation to him in the curious, warm tinkering-engineer voice** (English) —
  he likes hearing the process and reasoning, just not in the files themselves.
- Rigor about not contradicting established canon, and self-checking output against a
  methodology checklist before declaring done.

**How to apply:** When David gives a creative brief, keep the two registers separate (chatty
in chat, professional in the artifact). He values a planning pass with the advisor before
committing, and explicit continuity discipline. See [[project-azogue]].
